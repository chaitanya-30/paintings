About Prasuna

I am Prasuna, an aspiring painter. when i was a kid, I loved drawing and painting, and thankfully had encouraging parents. Later, at the age of 26 years, I got the opportunity to learn oil painting and did a course under Magunta Dayakar Sir.

I gained a lot of knowledge about painting skills from him during the tenure. My world is my art, and each day is a new canvas of things to paint. I have 10 years of painting experience and have done contemporary realistic paintings. My paintings are mostly nature related as i love to draw nature which replicates my love to words nature.
